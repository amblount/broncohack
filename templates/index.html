Empowering homeless with streamlined payments and job opportunities.

Promoting Donations

Promoting Donations Urban Wallet shrinks the communication gap between donors and the homeless. With the click of a button, any pedestrian can choose from different forms of payment to conveniently donate to a homeless person of their choice. The application allows for the homeless on the street to identify themselves with a QR code that the pedestrian can easily scan and choose from multiple forms of payment to donate. The donation will go directly to a shelter or soup kitchen in which the homeless person identifies with. This also encourages the homeless person to take advantage of the resources available to them when they come to the shelter to pick up their donation in cash.

About Us

Urban Wallet has been an established company for over five years during which we have facilitated over 400,000 dollars worth of donations and have placed over 500 homeless individuals with employment opportunities. Our company has been featured on CNN, New York Times, Fortune 100, Forbes 30 Companies Under 30, and much more.
